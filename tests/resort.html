Our hotel in Olang broadens the horizon

Between heaven and earth, in a picturesque place amidst the Dolomites, lies our unique hotel in Olang . A place of peace. A place of joy. A place of inspiration. In front of our hotel in Olang, the Alpin Panorama Hotel Hubertus: the imposing Dolomite peaks with their impressive rock faces, who are silent witnesses to the forces of nature that shaped this special region. Borders disappear against this backdrop; borders between architecture and landscape, heaven and earth, man and nature. Take a break in our hotel in Olang and become one with yourself and with the surrounding nature.

Our hotel in Val Pusteria/Pustertal, your happy place

Your time is here and now. Here at Hotel Hubertus.

There are places where you feel at home right from the start. Places you cannot wait to visit again. One of these places is our hotel in Olang, the Alpin Panorama Hotel Hubertus. Inspired by the diversity of the natural landscapes that surround our hotel in Val Pusteria/Pustertal , we have turned our once small mountain inn into a cosy holiday refuge where you can leave everyday life behind and devote yourself to the truly good things in life . Everything that counts is the here and now, is you and your wellbeing. Lean back and let this unique place of peace and silence take effect on you. The fresh mountain air ensures clear, carefree thoughts, the sun provides unclouded hours of bliss.

NEW: Heaven & Hell Sky Spa

The wind of change is blowing at our hotel: We opened our Sky Spa with the evocative name Heaven & Hell , an architectural masterwork that literally turns wellness upside down. You can look forward to heavenly hours of relaxation across two levels in three new whirlpools – the lie-down, seated, and nude whirlpools – as well as hellishly good sweat sessions in the two new saunas. When it’s time for cool refreshment, the ice room awaits. A walkway takes you from the new spa area to the relaxation room, which opens up new perspectives thanks to its large floor-to-ceiling windows.

Our hotel in Olang for your blissful break

Roots that give you wings

We are deeply rooted in our country. These roots inspire us again and again to treat you to an unforgettable stay. As a proud member of the Belvita Leading Wellnesshotels Südtirol  and Best Alpine Wanderhotels  hotel cooperations we pamper you on all levels. Watching the clouds pass by the mountain meadows that surround the Alpenreych Park , floating in the water of the Sky Pool, hiking on lonely paths, tramping through landscapes covered in deep snow, watching the sunrise on the summit, tasting a glass of wine, discovering the most diverse flavours, being pampered by the spa team – holiday happiness has many faces in our hotel in Olang. Experience them all and book  your time out in your happy place ! We look forward to your visit.
